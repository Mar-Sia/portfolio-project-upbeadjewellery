Pearl Bracelets

Unique & upcycled pearl bracelets 🌸 These bracelets are definitely more of a statement piece, but still dainty and delicate due to the interlocking strings of small beads. The bracelets have an adjustable extender chain to fit them beautifully around your wrist 😊 They extend from around 17-21cm. All of the beads and pearls are sourced and up-cycled from second-hand jewellery 🌱

Beaded Rings

Stretchy, stackable, beaded rings 🌞 They’re a unique and fun way to accessorize on the daily. Go for a minimalistic look with one ring, or all out by stacking the different colours together! 🌸 Each ring will vary very slightly in size due to the beads being upcycled from different pieces of jewellery ☺️. They are stretchy and will sit comfortably on the finger, but I would recommend choosing the larger size if you experience swelling or want a more loose fitting ring (it will still stay on the finger). The ring base is a clear, elastic jewellery cord and the beads come from a variety of upcycled jewellery pieces 🧿

Lizard Earrings

Super fun beaded lizard drop earrings 🧿 They are made with a very thin jewellery wire, so the tail and legs can be adjusted into different positions 🦎 Upcycled from an array of second-hand jewellery pieces 🌱
